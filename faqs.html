Frequently Asked Questions

Is it better to have maintenance done weekly or bi weekly?
In order to start seeing a difference on your property sooner than later it is recommended to have weekly maintenance.
What are some of the services that maintenance comes with ?
It all depends what you are looking for. It can be as simple as mowing your property, string trimming, and leaf blowing ; Or a little more detailed such as trimming shrubs, edging, weeding.
Do you guys only do landscape work?
We like to to consider ourselves as a team of handymen. We have expanded our profession further than just landscape. We do brick paving, drainage installations , gutter cleaning, retaining walls and much more.
